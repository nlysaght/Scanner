Directions

Our offices are conveniently located in the centre of Dublin at:

Ferry House, Lower Mount Street, Dublin 2, Ireland.

This location is easily accessed using a range of private and public transport options. You can plan your journey here.

Plane

Dublin Airport is approximately 35 minutes away by Taxi. Average fares are €35. Alternatively the Air Coach service stops in Merrion Square, 5 minutes walk from the office.

Train

Dart and mainline rail: 5 minutes walk from Grand Canal Dock railway station. 10 minutes walk from Pearse Street railway station.

Luas

15 minutes walk from the Charlemont stop on the green line.

Bus

Mount Street, Baggot Street and the Merrion Square area of the city are served by a wide variety of buses and are within minutes walk of the office. The Aircoach service (Ballsbridge/Donnybrook route, Schoolhouse Hotel) stops adjacent to our offices.

Car

Visitor car parking spaces are available in the basement car park. Please call for directions and to reserve a space. Metered street parking is also available directly outside the door.
